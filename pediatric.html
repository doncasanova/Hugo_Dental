Pediatric Dental Services

Pediatric dental services put the "family" in Hugo Dental Care's family dentistry. We take the fear out of children's dentistry and help children understand the meaning of good oral hygiene. We teach them how to brush properly, and help them learn what foods and beverages are good for their teeth.


The young children, pre-teens, and teenagers require different approaches in dealing with their behavior, guiding their dental growth and development, and helping them avoid future dental problems.


We offer complete dental care for children of all ages. (At times we refer very young children with unique dental needs to local pediatric dental specialists with whom we have a strong relationship.)


Pediatric services include the following:

  • Exams
  • Cleaning
  • X-rays
  • Fillings
