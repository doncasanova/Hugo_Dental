Prevention beats treatment ...
"an ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure."

At Hugo Dental Care, we are concerned as much about preventing dental issues as we are with treating dental problems (restorative services). Preventive care is the best assurance of healthy teeth and gums as people age, and thus we have made preventive care the primary focus of our practice. Current research has shown close links between gum disease and many systemic problems, ranging from cardiovascular disease to preterm births and underweight infants.


From early childhood to old age, Hugo Dental Care offers you a choice for compassionate and gentle dental care that keeps you smiling. We are proud of our dental hygiene department; their skillful assistance makes our preventive care services complete. Our dental hygienists are highly trained, and they will help you fully understand the best techniques for oral hygiene for both young children and older adults.


Preventive services include the following:

  • Dental Exams

    • We use Diagnodent laser cavity detection, visual inspection, oral cancer exams, periodontal exams, and all of the latest technology to help you make informed decisions about treatment options.

  • Teeth Cleaning
  • Bitewing X-Rays
  • Dental Exams
  • Full-Mouth X-Rays
  • Fluoride Treatments (for children under 16)

We are focused on gentle dental care for the entire family, children, teens, young and middle-aged adults, and senior citizens. Call to make an appointment.

VIP Care Plan

We understand that the costs of dental care can become burdensome for families without dental insurance. Thus, we offer a unique VIP Care Plan to keep preventive dental care affordable. We make is possible for you to give yourself and your family the gift of ongoing dental health.


Members of the VIP Care Plan make an easy monthly payment that is automatically charged to a credit card or deducted from a checking account. Membership in the plan includes all preventive care as well as discounts on all services and restorations.

VIP Care Plan membership includes the following benefits:

    Adults

  • 2 standard cleanings per year
  • 2 exams per year
  • 4 bitewing x-rays per year
  • Dental Exams
  • 1 full-mouth set of x-rays at start of membership and then one every five years
  • Fluoride Treatments (for children under 16)
  • 10% discount on all restorations and other services

    Children (17 and under)

  • 2 cleanings per year
  • 2 exams per year
  • 2-4 bitewing x-rays per year
  • 2 fluoride treatments per year
  • 36% discount on sealants for permanent molars
  • 10% discount on all restorations and other services

    Cost for Membership

  • Adults - $30/month
  • Children - $25/month

Payments are automatically deducted from a credit card or checking account on the day of the month that you specify. The VIP Care Plan is a one-year membership, renewable automatically unless you notify Hugo Dental Care in writing to discontinue membership. Payments made for membership services are non-refundable, even if services are not used.
