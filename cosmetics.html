Cosmetic Services

Hugo Dental Care offers cosmetic dental services to rejuvenate your smile. Just as a facelift improves a profile, cosmetic dentistry gives your smile new life. We offer cosmetic services that include the following.


  • Whitening

    • Coffee, smoking, age, and some medications can cause tooth discoloration. Our whitening techniques are superior to store-bought teeth whitening products.

  • Makeovers

    • Veneers and crowns give teeth a new look that is as natural as possible (with no metal). Porcelain veneers restore the appearance of chipped, broken, stained, or uneven front teeth. Something as simple as enamel re-contouring can create the illusion of straighter teeth.
