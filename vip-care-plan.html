VIP Care Plan

Give you and your family the gift of continued oral health!

Consistent and timely dental care is so important to your dental, as well as over all, health. At Hugo Dental Care we want to make it easy, convenient and affordable as possible for our patients to maintain a high quality of oral health. To help achieve this goal we have created the VIP CARE PLAN for those patients not covered under a dental insurance plan. Members of the VIP CARE PLAN make an easy monthly payment that is automatically deducted from either a credit card or checking account. Membership includes all preventative care as well as discounts on all services and restorations.


VIP Care Plan members will recieve the following:

Adults:

  • 2 standard cleanings per year
  • 2 exams per year
  • 4 bitewing x-rays per year
  • 1 full mouth set of x-rays at the start of your membership and then one every 5 years
  • 10% discount on all restorations and services

Children (17 and under)

  • 2 cleanings per year
  • 2 exams per year
  • 2-4 bitewing x-rays per year
  • 2 fluoride treatments per year
  • 36% discount on sealants for permanent molars
  • 10% discount on all restorations and services

Cost of Membership

Adults $30.00/month       Children $25.00/month

Payments will be automatically deducted from a credit card or checking account on the day of the month that you specify. The VIP CARE PLAN is a one year membership. We will renew your membership automatically unless we are notified in writing to discontinue membership. If membership services go unused by members there will not be a refund of payments made.
